=Description=
<br>
OpenVAS is an open source vulnerability management tool. It is used to identify and fix vulnerabilities in networks and servers. The tool can be used to scan for vulnerabilities in network services, operating systems, and applications. OpenVAS can be used to schedule scans, track vulnerabilities, and generate reports. The tool is available for Windows, Linux, and OS X.
