=Description=
<br>
Password state is a secure, centralized password management system which helps organizations to manage their passwords in a secure and efficient manner. With Password state, Organizations can have full control over their passwords, with the ability to set granular permissions, and track password usage. Password state also provides auditing and reporting capabilities, so that Organizations can keep track of who has access to what passwords, and when they accessed them.
