=Description=
<br>
The tool Find Emergency Resources from Consumer Reports helps you locate emergency resources near you in the event of a natural disaster or other crisis. You can search for resources by keyword, location, and type of resource. The tool also includes links to resources from government agencies and non-profit organizations.
