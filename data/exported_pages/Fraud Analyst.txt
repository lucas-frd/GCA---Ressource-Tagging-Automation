=Description=
<br>
The Fraud Analyst is a tool that helps businesses detect and prevent fraud. It uses data analytics to identify patterns of fraud and helps businesses automate the process of fraud detection. The Fraud Analyst also includes a A.I. chatbot that helps businesses communicate with their customers to resolve fraud disputes.
