=Description=
<br>
EmpowerID is an identity and access management tool that provides a single platform for managing users, credentials, and access to resources. EmpowerID consolidates multiple identity stores into a single repository, making it easy to manage multiple identities and access control across an organization. EmpowerID also provides real-time provisioning and de-provisioning of user accounts, making it easy to add and remove users from the system as needed.
