Authenticator App is a 2Stable application that allows users to generate and manage two-factor authentication (2FA) codes for their accounts on websites and applications that support 2FA. The app supports multiple 2FA methods, including time-based one-time password (TOTP) codes, push notifications, and one-time codes.
