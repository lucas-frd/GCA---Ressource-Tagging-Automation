OSSEC (Open Source Host-based Intrusion Detection System) is an open-source software project that was founded and is managed by Trend Micro, a global leader in cybersecurity solutions. The project is based at https://ossec.github.io/ and is dedicated to providing free and open-source security tools to the community.

The organization has a team of dedicated developers and contributors who work together to continually enhance and improve the Ossec platform. The team is composed of experts in the fields of security and network defense
