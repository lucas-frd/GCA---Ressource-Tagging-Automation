CrowdStrike Managed Threat Hunting is a service offered by CrowdStrike, a leading provider of cloud-delivered endpoint protection. The organization is focused on proactively detecting and responding to advanced cyber threats, including persistent and emerging threats that traditional security solutions may miss.

This service works in conjunction with the CrowdStrike Falcon platform, which leverages artificial intelligence and machine learning to continuously monitor and protect endpoints, both in the cloud and on-premises. The Managed Threat Hunting team is comprised of experienced security professionals
