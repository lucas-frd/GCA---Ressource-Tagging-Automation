The organization "How to Protect Your Social Media Accounts" is a blog run by the cybersecurity company McAfee. As a global leader in the cybersecurity industry, McAfee is dedicated to helping individuals and businesses protect their digital lives from the ever-evolving threat landscape.

The blog is specifically focused on providing readers with information and tips on how to safeguard their social media accounts from cyber threats.
