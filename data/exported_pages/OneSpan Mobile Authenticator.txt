=Description=
<br>
Mobile Authenticator is a mobile app that allows users to securely login to their OneSpan account using their fingerprint, face, or a passcode. OneSpan Mobile Authenticator also allows users to generate one-time passcodes for Two-Factor Authentication (2FA), and to securely store their OneSpan login credentials.
