=Description=
<br>
Secure Your Smart Speaker is a tool designed to help users secure their smart speakers. The tool provides users with a list of security measures to take, such as setting up a strong password, enabling two-factor authentication, and disabling guest access. The tool also provides guidance on what to do if a smart speaker is lost or stolen.
