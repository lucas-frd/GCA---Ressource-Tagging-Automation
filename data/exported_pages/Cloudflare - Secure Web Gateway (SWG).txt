Secure Web Gateway (SWG) is an organization based in Cloudflare that offers a comprehensive and advanced web security solution to businesses and individuals. The primary goal of SWG is to provide an all-in-one platform for protecting against web-based threats, including malware, ransomware, phishing attacks, and other malicious activities.
