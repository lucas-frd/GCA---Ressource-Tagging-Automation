The Ransomware Risk Assessment tool from Zscaler can help organizations assess their susceptibility to ransomware attacks. The tool provides a questionnaire that covers topics such as network security, user training, and backup and recovery procedures. After completing the questionnaire, the tool generates a report that includes recommendations for improving the organization's defenses against ransomware.
