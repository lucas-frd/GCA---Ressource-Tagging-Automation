GovPilot is an organization that specializes in providing digital transformation solutions for government entities. It was founded in 2012 and is headquartered in Hoboken, New Jersey. The company's main goal is to help government agencies improve their processes and services through the use of technology, while also ensuring data security and preventing cyber attacks.

The organization has identified seven key cybersecurity best practices that government agencies should implement to keep their data secure and prevent ransomware attacks.
