=Description=
<br>
This tool helps you update your Windows PC so that it is more secure. It offers four options for updating your PC: update all software, including Windows; update security software only; update Windows only; and do not update anything. It also provides links to instructions for updating your PC manually. This tool is designed to help you keep your PC more secure by ensuring that it is up to date with the latest security patches.
