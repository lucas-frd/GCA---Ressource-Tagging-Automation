=Description=
<br>
OpenIAM is an online tool that allows organizations to manage their digital identities. It provides a single platform for managing multiple identities and provides a variety of features to help organizations control access to their resources. OpenIAM also offers a variety of reports and analytics to help organizations understand their identity management practices.
