=Description=
<br>
Zilla Security is a cloud-based platform that helps organizations prevent identity-related data breaches. It does this by providing a central repository for all identity and security information, as well as a set of tools to help organizations manage and control access to this information. Zilla also assists organizations in meeting compliance requirements, by providing a platform for documenting and tracking compliance-related activities.
