=Description=
<br>
The SANS Institute has developed a tool called Ncat which is a network exploration and administration tool. It can be used for tasks such as port scanning,posing as a web server, transferring files, or connecting to a remote shell. Ncat is written in the C programming language and is available for free from the institute's website.
