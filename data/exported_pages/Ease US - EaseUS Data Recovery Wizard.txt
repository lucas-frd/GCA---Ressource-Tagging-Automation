EaseUS Data Recovery Wizard is a data recovery software developed by the Chinese technology company EaseUS. Founded in 2004, EaseUS has become a leader in the data management and protection sector, providing solutions for data backups, hard drive management, and data recovery.

The organization operates under the mission of enabling people to easily recover lost data and protect their precious files from unexpected data loss.
