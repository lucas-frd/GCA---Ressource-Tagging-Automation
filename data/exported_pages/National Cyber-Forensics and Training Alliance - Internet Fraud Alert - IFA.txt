Internet Fraud Alert (IFA) is a warning system that helps protect consumers from online fraud. It works by sharing information about known or suspected fraud with member agencies, who can then take action to prevent it. IFA is operated by the National Cyber-Forensics & Training Alliance (NCFTA), a non-profit organization that works to fight cybercrime.
