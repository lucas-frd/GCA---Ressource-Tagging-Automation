=Description=
<br>
NetworkMiner is a SANS Institute tool that can be used to monitor and analyze network traffic. It can be used to identify hosts and devices on a network, as well as to track traffic flows and collect forensic data. NetworkMiner can be used to investigate suspicious network activity, to troubleshoot network problems, or to simply monitor network traffic.
