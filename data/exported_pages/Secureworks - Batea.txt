The tool Batea found at https://github.com/delvelabs/batea is a Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) system that helps businesses to secure and manage their networks. It offers an advanced correlation engine that can identify and respond to threats in real time, as well as a range of other features such as asset discovery, incident response, and compliance management.
