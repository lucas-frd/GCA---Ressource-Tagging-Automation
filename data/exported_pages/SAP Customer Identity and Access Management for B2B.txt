SAP Customer Identity and Access Management for B2B (C4B) is a secure, cloud-based identity management solution that helps businesses manage customer identities and access to business applications. C4B provides a single, unified view of customer identities across multiple business applications, making it easy to manage customer access and identities. C4B also offers a robust set of security features to protect customer data, including multi-factor authentication, password management, and user activity monitoring.
