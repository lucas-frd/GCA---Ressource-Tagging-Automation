Lookout Security & Antivirus is a Lookout Mobile Security app that helps keep your Android device safe from viruses, malware, and other threats. It includes a virus scanner that can detect and remove malware, a malware removal tool that can help you remove malware from your device, and a phone locator that can help you find a lost or stolen device. Lookout Security & Antivirus is a free app, but it does include ads.
