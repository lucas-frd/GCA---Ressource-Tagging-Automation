NFT Now is an organization that is dedicated to educating and informing the public about the world of non-fungible tokens (NFTs). They aim to provide valuable resources and guides for those interested in the rapidly growing NFT market.

One of their informational articles, titled 'Scams Explained: What are Rug Pulls? Are They Crimes?' delves into the concept of rug pulls, a common scam tactic used in the NFT world.
