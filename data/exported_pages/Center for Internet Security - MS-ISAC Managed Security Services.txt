The MS-ISAC Managed Security Services is a division of Center for Internet Security (CIS) that specializes in providing comprehensive and advanced security solutions for organizations of all sizes. CIS is a nonprofit organization that was established in 2000 with the mission to identify, develop, and promote best practices for securing IT systems and data.

The MS-ISAC Managed Security Services offers a wide range of services such as Managed Security Monitoring, Intrusion Detection and Prevention, Vulnerability Management, and Incident Response.
