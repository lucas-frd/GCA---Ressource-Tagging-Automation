=Description=
<br>
Kismet is a rugged, low-cost, passive Wi-Fi radar designed to detect 802.11a/b/g/n traffic. It features an intuitive web interface that allows users to visualize their wireless landscape in real time. Kismet can be used to track down rogue access points, identify at-risk areas in your network, and monitor Wi-FiSampler/NetStumbler-compatible devices.
