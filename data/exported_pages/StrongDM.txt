StrongDM is a Dynamic Access Management platform that puts people first by giving technical staff a direct route to the critical infrastructure they need to be their most productive. End users enjoy frictionless access to all of their work resources, whether on-premises or in the cloud. StrongDM delivers complete visibility into how your resources are being accessed and by whom, so you can be confident that your data is always safe.
