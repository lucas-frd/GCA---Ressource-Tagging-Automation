Check Point Software Technologies Ltd. is a multinational company based in Tel Aviv, Israel that specializes in providing cybersecurity solutions. Founded in 1993, it has become one of the leading companies in the cybersecurity industry and currently serves over 100,000 organizations worldwide. The company offers a wide range of products, including network and endpoint security, cloud security, mobile security, and security management.

One of the products offered by Check Point is the Software-Defined Wide Area Network (SD-WAN) solution
