SafePaaS is a platform that enables organizations to securely store, share, and manage data in the cloud. It offers a variety of features that allow users to control access to data, set permissions, and audit activity. SafePaaS also provides a range of security and compliance features, such as encryption, two-factor authentication, and activity monitoring.
