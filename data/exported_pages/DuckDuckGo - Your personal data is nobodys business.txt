DuckDuckGo is a privacy-oriented internet search engine that aims to provide users with a secure and anonymous browsing experience. The organization was founded in 2008 by Gabriel Weinberg, a web entrepreneur and programmer. Its headquarters are located in Paoli, Pennsylvania, in the United States.

The primary mission of DuckDuckGo is to protect users' online privacy by not tracking or collecting their personal data, such as search history, IP addresses, or any other identifying information.
