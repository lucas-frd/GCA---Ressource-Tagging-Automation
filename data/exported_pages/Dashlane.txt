Dashlane is a password management tool that allows users to efficiently and securely manage their passwords. The tool uses a variety of features to ensure that passwords are stored securely and can be accessed easily when needed. Dashlane also offers a variety of other features, such as the ability to generate strong passwords and to share passwords with other users.
