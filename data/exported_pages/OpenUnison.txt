=Description=
<br>
OpenUnison is a tool that allows users to securely connect to their data and applications from any device, anywhere. It provides a centralized view of all the users and devices in an organization, as well as the ability to control access to data and applications. OpenUnison also includes a powerful user management system, which makes it easy to add and remove users, as well as to assign permissions and privileges.
