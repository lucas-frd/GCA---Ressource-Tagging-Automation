=Description=
<br>
Authentiq is a tool that allows users to authenticate themselves and access online services in a secure and convenient way. It uses a unique ID number that is assigned to each user, and a password that is known only to the user. This allows users to access their account from any computer or mobile device, without having to remember multiple passwords. Authentiq is a free service, and it is available to anyone who wishes to use it.
