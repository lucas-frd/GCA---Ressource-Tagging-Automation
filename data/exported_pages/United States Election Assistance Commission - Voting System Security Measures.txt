The tool Voting System Security Measures is a United States Election Assistance Commission document that outlines security measures for voting systems. The document covers topics such as physical security, system security, and election security. The document includes a list of recommended security measures for each topic. The recommended measures are meant to improve the security of voting systems and to protect the integrity of elections.
