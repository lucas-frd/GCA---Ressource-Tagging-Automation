The SANS Institute is a tool responder found at https://www.sans.org/tools/?per-page=100. The site provides a variety of tools for security professionals, including a tool for searching the web for security information, a tool for managing passwords, and a tool for encrypting files. The site also provides a forum for discussion and an online store for purchasing security products.
