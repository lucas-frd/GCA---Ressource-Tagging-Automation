=Description=
<br>
Azure IoT Hub is a cloud-based platform that helps you connect, monitor, and manage IoT devices. With IoT Hub, you can collect data from your devices and send commands to them. You can also use IoT Hub to create and manage device identities, and to set up rules for routing data and messages. IoT Hub is available in three pricing tiers: Basic, Standard, and Premium.
