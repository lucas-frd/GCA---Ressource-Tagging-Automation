PortalGuard is a password management and authentication tool that helps organizations improve their security posture by protecting against password-related attacks. PortalGuard provides a central repository for storing and managing passwords, as well as a set of tools for authenticating users and granting them access to specific resources. It offers a variety of features, including the ability to set up strong password policies, enforce two-factor authentication, and monitor activity for suspicious behavior.
