=Description=
<br>
Maltego is a tool used for gathering intelligence about people, organizations, and relationships. It is also used for conducting link analysis. Maltego can be used to find information about a person's online presence, as well as to find relationships between people. Maltego is a powerful tool for doing investigative work and can be used by law enforcement, private investigators, and businesses.
