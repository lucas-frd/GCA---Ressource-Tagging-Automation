The tool Update Your iPhone, iPad, or iPod Touch helps users check for and install updates for their Apple devices. The tool first checks to see if there are any new updates available for the device, and then guides the user through the process of installing the update. The tool also provides instructions on how to update other Apple devices, such as the Apple Watch and Mac computers.
