Viasat is a global communications company that provides high-speed satellite internet services. The company was founded in 1986 and is currently headquartered in Carlsbad, California, with its satellite internet division known as Exede based in Denver, Colorado.

Viasat's primary focus is on providing high-speed satellite internet connectivity to underserved and remote areas where traditional broadband options are limited.
