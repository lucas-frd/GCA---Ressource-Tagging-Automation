=Description=
<br>
The Splunk Training is a web-based application that helps users learn how to use Splunk software. The app provides a series of tutorials and exercises to help users master Splunk's search, reporting, and analysis features. The app also includes a simulated environment that allows users to practice using Splunk without having to install the software on their own computer.
