Wire is a secure communication and collaboration platform. The organization was founded in Switzerland during 2014 by Alan Duric, Jonathan Christensen, and Priidu Zilmer, with a vision to create a secure messaging platform that would prioritize user privacy and security. Since then, the organization has gained recognition for its innovative approach to encrypted communication and has become a popular tool for businesses and individuals alike.
