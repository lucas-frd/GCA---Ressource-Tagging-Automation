Micro Focus Identity and Access Management (IAM) is a tool that helps organizations manage and secure user identities and access to resources. IAM includes features such as user provisioning, password management, Single Sign-On (SSO), and access control. With IAM, organizations can improve security and compliance, while reducing costs and improving efficiency.
