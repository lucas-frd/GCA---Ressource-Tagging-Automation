This tool provides a few simple steps to protect yourself when using public Wi-Fi. First, it recommends encrypting your traffic with a VPN. Second, it suggests only using websites that are HTTPS-enabled. Finally, it advises against sharing sensitive information or downloading anything while on public Wi-Fi. By following these steps, you can help protect yourself from data theft and other malicious activities.
