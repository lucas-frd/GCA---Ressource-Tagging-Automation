=Description=
<br>
mPass is a secure login tool that allows users to access their favorite online services with a single username and password. mPass alleviates the need to remember multiple login credentials by securely storing them in an encrypted format on the user's device. When the user attempts to log in to a service that is supported by mPass, they are prompted to enter their mPass username and password.
