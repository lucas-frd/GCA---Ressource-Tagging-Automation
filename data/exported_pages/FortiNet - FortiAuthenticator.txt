=Description=
<br>
FortiAuthenticator is a one-stop solution for user authentication, providing powerful centralized management for Fortinet's industry-leading security platforms. Offering support for a wide range of authentication methods, including two-factor authentication, FortiAuthenticator simplifies the deployment and administration of authentication across the network. Additionally, FortiAuthenticator provides detailed audit logging and supports integration with a wide range of third-party identity management solutions.
