The SecZetta Identity Consolidation Solution is a consolidated solution that helps organizations manage and monitor privileged access across all environments. It provides a centralized repository for all identity data, making it easy to review and authorize access. The solution also includes a built-in workflow engine that automates the process of requesting and approving access.
