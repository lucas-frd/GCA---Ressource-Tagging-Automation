=Description=
<br>
GRC is a cloud-based solution that enables organizations to automate and simplify their compliance management processes. It provides a central repository for compliance-related information and documentation, and features a workflow management tool to track and assign tasks to specific users. The solution also includes a reporting tool to generate compliance reports on demand.
