=Description=
<br>
HashiCorp's Vault is a tool for managing secrets, such as passwords, API keys, and certificates. Vault provides a secure, centralised store for secrets, and can be used to generate and revoke secrets on-demand. Vault is designed to be highly scalable and can be used to manage secrets for thousands of users.
