The tool Security Challenges in Web3 Gaming and GameFi is a Halborn blog post that explores the potential security risks associated with gaming on the decentralized web. The post discusses the decentralized nature of web3 gaming and how this could lead to problems with game integrity and cheating. It also discusses the lack of regulation in this area and how this could lead to unregulated gambling.
