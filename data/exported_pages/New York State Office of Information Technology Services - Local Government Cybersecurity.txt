The Local Government Cybersecurity organization is a division of the New York State Office of Information Technology Services (ITS). Its main focus is on protecting local government agencies in New York State from cyber threats and attacks. The organization was established to provide resources and support to local government entities as they work towards improving their cybersecurity posture.

The ITS is responsible for maintaining the statewide IT infrastructure and providing technological solutions to various state agencies, including local governments.
