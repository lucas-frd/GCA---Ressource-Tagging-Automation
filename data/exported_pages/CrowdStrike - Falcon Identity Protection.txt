Falcon Identity Protection is a subscription bundle offered by the cybersecurity organization CrowdStrike. This bundle is designed to provide robust and comprehensive protection against identity-based cyber attacks.

CrowdStrike is a global leader in endpoint and cloud security and is known for its advanced cybersecurity solutions. The organization was founded in 2011 and is headquartered in Sunnyvale, California. CrowdStrike has a global presence with offices in the United States, Australia, India, and the United Kingdom, among others.
