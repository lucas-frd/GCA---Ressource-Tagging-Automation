=Description=
<br>
Ettercap is a free and open source network security tool for man-in-the-middle attacks on LAN. It can be used to sniff traffic on a network, capture credentials, and even inject custom payloads into connections. Ettercap is a versatile tool that can be used for a variety of tasks, making it a valuable tool for any security professional.
