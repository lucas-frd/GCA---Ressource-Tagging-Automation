=Description=
<br>
PasswordWrench 2-Factor Authentication is a tool that allows users to securely store and manage their passwords. It uses a two-factor authentication system to ensure that only the authorized user can access the passwords. The tool also allows users to generate strong passwords and to view and manage their password history.
