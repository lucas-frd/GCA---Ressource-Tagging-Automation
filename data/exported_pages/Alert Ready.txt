Alert Ready is a Canadian organization that focuses on providing timely and effective emergency alerts to the public. It is a partnership between federal, provincial, and territorial emergency management organizations, as well as public broadcasters and telecommunication companies. The organization is based in Alert Ready, which is a national emergency alert system in Canada.

The main goal of Alert Ready is to ensure that Canadians have access to critical and potentially life-saving information during an emergency situation.
