The Nymi is a wearable device that uses your unique cardiac rhythm to authenticate your identity. It consists of a sensing platform that attaches to your wrist and a companion app that you use to complete the enrollment process. Once enrolled, you can use the Nymi to sign in to your computer, unlock your phone, and make payments.
