The tool SecureITSource is a platform that helps organisations to manage and secure their IT infrastructure. It provides a centralised view of all the IT assets and devices in an organisation, and allows organisations to track and manage their security posture. The tool also provides a range of features to help organisations to secure their IT infrastructure, including asset management, vulnerability management, incident response, and security analytics.
