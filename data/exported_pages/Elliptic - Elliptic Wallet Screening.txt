Elliptic Wallet Screening is a service that helps cryptocurrency businesses comply with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations. The service screens transactions in real-time to identify suspicious activity and Flag high-risk addresses. Elliptic also provides a risk management platform that enables businesses to track their exposure to cryptocurrency risk and make informed decisions about which coins to accept.
