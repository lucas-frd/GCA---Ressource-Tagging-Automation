AVG antivirus pro is a great tool for PC that offers protection against viruses, malware, and other online threats. It has a real-time scanner that scans your computer for threats and blocks them if they are found. It also has a quarantine area where it stores threats that it has found so that you can remove them later. It has a schedule function so that you can scan your computer at certain times, and it also has a self-protection mode so that it can protect itself
