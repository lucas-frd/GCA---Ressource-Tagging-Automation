=Description=
<br>
9Star Elastic SSO is an access management application that provides a single sign-on solution for enterprises. It offers a centralized login portal for users to access all their applications with one set of credentials. The tool also provides a self-service password management feature to allow users to reset their own passwords. Additionally, 9Star Elastic SSO integrates with other 9Star products to provide a complete identity and access management solution.
