=Description=
<br>
Arnica UnifiedLogon is a solution that allows organizations to securely authenticate users across multiple systems using a single set of credentials. The solution provides a centralized platform for managing user identities and passwords, and offers a variety of features to streamline the login process and improve security. UnifiedLogon integrates with existing identity management systems and can be deployed on-premises or in the cloud.
