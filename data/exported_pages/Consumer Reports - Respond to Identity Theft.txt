=Description=
<br>
The Respond to Identity Theft tool is a free online resource from Consumer Reports that helps victims of identity theft take action to recover their stolen identities. The tool provides step-by-step instructions for filing a police report, contacting credit bureaus, and taking other steps to recover from identity theft. The tool also includes links to helpful resources, such as a sample police report and a list of where to send credit bureau disputes.
