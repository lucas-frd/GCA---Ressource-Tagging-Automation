=Description=
<br>
The FCC Smartphone Security Checker is a tool designed to help people ensure that their smartphones are secure. The tool provides a list of security measures that people can take to protect their smartphones, as well as a list of potential threats to smartphones and what to do if a smartphone is lost or stolen. The tool also includes a list of resources that people can use to learn more about smartphone security.
