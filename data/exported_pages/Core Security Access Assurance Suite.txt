Core Security Access Assurance Suite is a centralized solution that provides comprehensive visibility and control over access to critical data and systems. It includes a range of modules that can be deployed according to an organization's needs, including user provisioning, identity and access management, authentication, authorization, and auditing. The suite also includes a centralized administration console and a reporting engine to help organizations track and monitor access to their systems and data.
