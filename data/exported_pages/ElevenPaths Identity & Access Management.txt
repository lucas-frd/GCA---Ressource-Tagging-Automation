The ElevenPaths Identity & Access Management tool is a complete solution for managing and protecting user identities and data. It offers a centralized platform for managing users, passwords, and permissions, as well as providing advanced features such as two-factor authentication and single sign-on. The tool is designed to work with a variety of applications and systems, making it a versatile solution for managing access to corporate resources.
