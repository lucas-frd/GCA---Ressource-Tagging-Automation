CrowdStrike Cloud Security Services is a division of the cybersecurity company CrowdStrike, based in Sunnyvale, California. This division focuses on providing cloud-based security solutions for businesses of all sizes, ranging from small startups to large enterprises.

The organization offers a comprehensive suite of services to protect against a wide range of threats, including malware, ransomware, and insider attacks. These services are designed to help organizations secure their cloud environments and ensure the safety of their data and applications.
