=Description=
<br>
RoboForm is a secure and easy-to-use password manager and form filler that stores your passwords and personal information in a secure, encrypted database. You can access your RoboForm data from any computer or mobile device, and RoboForm can automatically fill in your passwords and personal information on web forms. RoboForm also includes a built-in password generator, so you can create strong, unique passwords for all your online accounts.
