The Electronic Identification and Trust Services (eIDAS) Regulation is a European Union regulation that establishes a framework for electronic identification and trust services (eIDAS) in the EU. The eIDAS Regulation aims to create a single digital market for eIDAS in the EU and to increase trust in online transactions.
