Falcon Enterprise is a comprehensive security bundle offered by CrowdStrike, a leading provider of cloud-delivered endpoint protection. CrowdStrike was founded in 2011 by two former McAfee executives and has gained global recognition for its innovative and effective approach to cybersecurity.

The Falcon Enterprise bundle is designed to provide organizations with complete protection against modern and sophisticated cyber threats. It is a fully cloud-based solution that offers advanced endpoint protection, threat intelligence, and round-the-clock monitoring and response capabilities.
