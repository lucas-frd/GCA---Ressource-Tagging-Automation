SOTI Identity is an enterprise-grade mobile identity management solution that helps organizations securely manage and provision mobile devices and apps. It provides a centralized management console for provisioning, managing, and monitoring devices and apps. It also offers a variety of features to help organizations keep their data secure, including data encryption, remote wiping, and device and app blacklisting. SOTI Identity is available as a cloud-based or on-premises solution.
