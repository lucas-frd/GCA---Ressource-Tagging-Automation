=Description=
<br>
KeeWeb is a free, open-source password manager that provides a secure way to store and manage your passwords. It uses AES-256 encryption to keep your passwords safe, and provides an easy-to-use interface for managing them. You can create multiple password databases, and each database can be encrypted with a different key. KeeWeb also supports Two-Factor Authentication, which adds an extra layer of security to your account.
