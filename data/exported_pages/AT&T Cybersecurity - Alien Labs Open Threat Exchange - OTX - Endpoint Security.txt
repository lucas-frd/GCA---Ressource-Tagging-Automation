=Description=
<br>
The Alien Labs Open Threat Exchange is a platform that allows users to share information about cybersecurity threats in order to better protect themselves and their networks. The platform includes a variety of features such as a threat feed, a repository of malware samples, and a dashboard that allows users to see which threats are most active. The OTX platform is free to use and is open to anyone who wishes to contribute.
