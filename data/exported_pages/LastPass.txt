=Description=

LastPass is a secure, cloud-based password manager that helps users manage their passwords and other sensitive information. LastPass encrypts user data and stores it in a secure server, making it accessible only to the user who created the account. LastPass also offers a variety of features to make password management easier, such as the ability to generate strong passwords, autofill forms, and share passwords with others.
