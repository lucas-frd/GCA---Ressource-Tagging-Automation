The Social Media Cyber-Vandalism Toolkit is a Digital.gov resource that helps organizations prepare for, respond to, and recover from cyber-vandalism attacks on social media. The Toolkit includes guidance on identifying and mitigating risks, developing incident response plans, and conducting crisis communication. It also provides information on how to report cyber-vandalism incidents to law enforcement and digital forensics experts.
