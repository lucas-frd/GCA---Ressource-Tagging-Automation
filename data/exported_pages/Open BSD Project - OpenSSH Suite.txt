OpenSSH is a free and open-source suite of tools used for remotely accessing and managing a network of computers. The suite includes the core components of the OpenSSH server and client, as well as a number of supporting tools and utilities. The suite is typically used to securely connect to and manage remote systems, but can also be used for local system administration tasks.
