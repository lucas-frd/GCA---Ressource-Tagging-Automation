Crystal Investigations Services is a blockchain analysis tool that allows users to investigate the Elixir blockchain. The tool provides users with a variety of data points that can be used to help assess the risk of transactions and addresses on the blockchain. Crystal also offers a number of other features, including a block explorer and a transaction explorer.
