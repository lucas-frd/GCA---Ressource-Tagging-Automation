Secureworks is a cybersecurity firm that provides a variety of services, including threat intelligence, incident response, and vulnerability management. They also offer a tool called Vane2, which is a open-source data visualization platform. Vane2 allows users to see relationships between different types of data, such as IP addresses, domain names, and malicious activity. This can be helpful in identifying patterns and trends in cyber activity.
