Broadcom Symantec Network Detection and Response is an organization that focuses on providing cutting-edge solutions to protect and defend against cyber threats. It is a subsidiary of Broadcom, a leading global technology company that offers a wide range of products and services in the areas of networking, storage, software, and semiconductors.

The primary objective of Broadcom Symantec Network Detection and Response is to provide advanced cybersecurity solutions that enable businesses to effectively detect, investigate, and respond to cyber threats in real-time
