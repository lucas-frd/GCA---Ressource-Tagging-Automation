Secure Your Baby Monitor is a tool from Consumer Reports that helps parents secure their baby monitors. The tool provides step-by-step instructions on how to choose and set up a baby monitor, how to choose a safe location for the monitor, and how to keep the monitor secure. The tool also includes a list of common security risks and how to avoid them.
