=Description=
<br>
Radiant Logic is a data integration and identity management platform that enables organizations to connect to any data source, automate data collection and normalization, and turn data into actionable insights. The platform includes a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to connect to data sources, visualize data, and create reports and dashboards. Radiant Logic also offers a variety of cloud-based services that make it easy to deploy and manage data integration and identity management projects.
