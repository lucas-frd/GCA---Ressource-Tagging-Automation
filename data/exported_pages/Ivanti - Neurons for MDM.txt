Ivanti Neurons for MDM is a set of machine learning models that are designed to work with Ivanti's MDM solution. The models are trained on data from Ivanti's customers, and they are able to provide predictions and recommendations for Ivanti's MDM solution. The models are constantly learning and improving, and they are designed to provide the best possible experience for Ivanti's customers.
