Cryptojacking Explained is a resource provided by CSO, a leading information security website for business professionals. The purpose of Cryptojacking Explained is to educate individuals and organizations about the increasingly prevalent threat of cryptojacking, a form of cyberattack that exploits computer processing power to mine cryptocurrencies without the owner's permission or knowledge.
