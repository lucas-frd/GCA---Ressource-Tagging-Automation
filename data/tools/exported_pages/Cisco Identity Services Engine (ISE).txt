Cisco Identity Services Engine (ISE) is a network security management platform developed by Cisco Systems. It provides identity-based security policies and access control for network devices and endpoints. The purpose of Cisco ISE is to enable organizations to create and enforce security policies across their entire network, in order to protect their assets and data from unauthorized access.
