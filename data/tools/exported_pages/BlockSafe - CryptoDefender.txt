CryptoDefender is a tool that helps protect users from online threats such as phishing attacks and malware. It does this by providing a secure browser extension that blocks malicious websites and provides warnings about potential threats. CryptoDefender also includes a password manager and two-factor authentication to help keep users' accounts safe. In addition, the tool provides a variety of other security features such as a VPN and a secure email service.
