=Description=
<br>
GRR Rapid Response is a free and open source incident response tool that can be used to rapidly triage and investigate incidents. It is designed to be run on a live system, and can be used to collect data for forensic analysis. GRR Rapid Response is scriptable, and can be used to automatically respond to incidents.
