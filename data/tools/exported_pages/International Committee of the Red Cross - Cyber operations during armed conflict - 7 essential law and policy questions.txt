Cyber operations during armed conflict have become increasingly prevalent and complex in modern warfare. The use of technology and the internet has revolutionized the ways in which armed conflicts are fought, and it has raised several important law and policy questions. The International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) has identified seven essential law and policy questions that must be considered in relation to cyber operations during armed conflict.
