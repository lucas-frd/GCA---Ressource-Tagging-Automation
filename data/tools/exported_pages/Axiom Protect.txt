=Description=
<br>
Axiom Protect is a distributed ledger technology (DLT) tool that allows users to create and manage digital assets, including digital currencies, in a secure and transparent manner. The tool uses a variety of cryptographic techniques to ensure the security of digital assets and to prevent fraud and double-spending. Axiom Protect is also scalable, meaning that it can support a large number of transactions per second. The tool is designed to be easy to use, even for non-technical users.
