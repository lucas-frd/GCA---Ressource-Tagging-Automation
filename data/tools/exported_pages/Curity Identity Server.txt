The Curity Identity Server is an identity management tool that helps organizations to securely manage and protect their digital identities. It provides a centralized repository for storing and managing identity data, and enables organizations to quickly and easily provision new identities and access to applications and services. The Curity Identity Server also offers a range of features to help organizations meet their compliance requirements, including support for Single Sign-On (SSO) and multi-factor authentication (MFA).
