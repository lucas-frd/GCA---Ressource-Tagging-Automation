=Description=
<br>
The consumer reports Turn On Your Mac's Firewall is a guide that helps Mac users turn on their computer's firewall. The guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to access the firewall settings and how to enable the firewall. The guide also includes a list of suggested firewall settings for different types of users.
