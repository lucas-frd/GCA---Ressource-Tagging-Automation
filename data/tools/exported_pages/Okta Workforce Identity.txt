=Description=
<br>
Okta Workforce Identity is a cloud-based solution that allows organizations to manage and secure their employee identities. It includes features such as user provisioning, identity management, and Single Sign-On (SSO). Workforce Identity is designed to work with Okta's other products, such as Okta Identity Cloud and Okta Access Gateway, to provide a complete identity and access management solution.
