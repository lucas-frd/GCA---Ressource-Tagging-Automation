Recuva is a data recovery software company founded in 2007 and based in London, United Kingdom. The company is a subsidiary of the popular computer optimization and security software company, Piriform. Recuva specializes in developing powerful data recovery tools that help individuals and businesses to retrieve lost or deleted files and folders from various devices, including computers, external hard drives, and even digital cameras.

The main goal of Recuva is to provide an efficient and user-friendly solution for data recovery.
