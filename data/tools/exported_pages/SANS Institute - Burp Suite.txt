=Description=
<br>
Burp Suite is a SANS Institute-approved web application testing tool. It is a Java-based platform that provides users with a wide range of features to help them assess the security of web applications. Burp Suite is available in both a free and paid version. The free version is limited to assessing web applications with a maximum of 10 URLs.
