=Description=
<br>
Authress is a multi-factor authentication tool that allows users to securely access their accounts and services using a variety of different methods. This includes traditional password-based authentication, as well as more modern methods such as biometrics and one-time codes. Authress also offers a variety of features to help organisations manage and secure their authentication processes, including support for multi-factor authentication policies, Single Sign-On (SSO), and passwordless login.
