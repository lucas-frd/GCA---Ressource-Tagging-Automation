Cidaas is an online platform that provides identity management and access management services. It offers a variety of features such as Social Login, Two-Factor Authentication, User Management, and Single Sign-On. Cidaas is a cloud-based platform that is designed to be scalable and secure. It is used by organizations of all sizes, from small businesses to large enterprises.
