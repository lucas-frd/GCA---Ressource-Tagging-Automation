The IDCARE Apps & Tools is designed to provide individuals with a range of resources to help them better understand and manage their identity. The site includes a range of tools, including a password manager, an identity restoration checklist, and a fraud monitoring service. The site also provides information on a range of identity theft and fraud prevention topics.
