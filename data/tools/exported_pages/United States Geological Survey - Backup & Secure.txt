Backup & Secure is an integral part of the United States Geological Survey (USGS) data management program. It is responsible for ensuring the safety and security of all data collected and processed by the USGS. This includes data from various research and monitoring programs, as well as data acquired from external sources.

The main goal of Backup & Secure is to prevent the loss of critical data due to system failures, disasters, or human error.
