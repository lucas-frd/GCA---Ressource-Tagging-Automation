The NCSC's Cyber Security Small Charity Guide is designed to help small charities protect themselves against cyber attacks. The guide covers a range of topics, including how to choose and implement appropriate security measures, how to create a good security culture within the organisation, and how to respond to a security incident. The guide also includes a number of case studies and examples of good practice.
