=Description=
<br>
Rippling is a tool that helps manage and automate the process of onboarding new employees. It allows managers to quickly create an account for a new hire, and then automatically generates the necessary paperwork and sets up email accounts and access to company resources. Rippling also allows managers to keep track of all onboarding progress in one place, and provides a variety of reports and analytics to help improve the process.
