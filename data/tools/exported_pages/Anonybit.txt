=Description=
<br>
Anonybit is an online tool that allows users to anonymously browse the web. It does this by encrypting the user's traffic and routing it through a series of servers that mask the user's IP address. Anonybit also blocks cookies and other tracking mechanisms that could be used to identify the user.
