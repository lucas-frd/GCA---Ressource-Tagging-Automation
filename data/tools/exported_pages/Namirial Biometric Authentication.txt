=Description=
<br>
The Namirial Biometric Authentication is a system that utilizes biometric data to authenticate individuals. The system uses either a person’s fingerprint or iris scan to verify their identity. This verification process is completed by matching the biometric data to a record in the system’s database. Once the individual’s identity is confirmed, they are granted access to the desired service or resource.

The Namirial Biometric Authentication system is designed to be highly secure
