ManageEngine M365 Manager Plus is a centralized, comprehensive admin console for Office 365 that simplifies and automates day-to-day management tasks of Office 365. It provides a single console to manage users, mailboxes, licenses, groups, and permissions. You can also create custom reports, add and manage multiple domains, and delegate administrative tasks to others.
