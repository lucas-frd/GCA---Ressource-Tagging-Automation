The tool Security Awareness is a service provided by Crypto.com that helps users learn about and keep up to date with security threats. The service provides a variety of resources, including articles, videos, and presentations, that cover topics such as online security, identity theft, and phishing scams. The service also offers a monthly newsletter that highlights new security threats and tips for staying safe online.
