=Description=
<br>
The Crest Incident Response tool is a web-based application that enables organizations to plan for and respond to incidents. It provides a framework for incident response and guides users through the process of identifying, containing, and recovering from incidents. The tool also includes a library of resources, including a incident response plan template, checklists, and best practices.
