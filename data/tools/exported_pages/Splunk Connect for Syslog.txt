=Description=
<br>
Splunk Connect for Syslog is a Splunk app that allows you to collect and index syslog data from remote systems. It uses the syslog-ng application to collect syslog data and send it to a Splunk server for indexing. The app provides a web interface for viewing and searching your syslog data.
