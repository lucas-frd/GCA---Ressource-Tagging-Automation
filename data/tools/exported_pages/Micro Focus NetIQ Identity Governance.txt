Micro Focus NetIQ Identity Governance is a micro focus identity and access management product. It allows organizations to manage user access to systems and data, as well as provides a mechanism for auditing and compliance. The product includes a variety of features such as role-based access control, workflow management, and integration with third-party products. It is designed to work with Micro Focus eDirectory and other LDAP-based directory services.
