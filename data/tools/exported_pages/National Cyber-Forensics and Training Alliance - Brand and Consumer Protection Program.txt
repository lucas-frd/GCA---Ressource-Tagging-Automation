The Brand and Consumer Protection Program is a tool from the National Cyber-Forensics and Training Alliance that helps companies protect their brands and consumers from online threats. The program provides companies with resources to help them identify, track, and manage online threats, as well as training to help employees stay up-to-date on the latest cyber threats.
