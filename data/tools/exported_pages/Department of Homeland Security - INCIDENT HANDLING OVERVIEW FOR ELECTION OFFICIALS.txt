=Description=
<br>
The tool, INCIDENT HANDLING OVERVIEW FOR ELECTION OFFICIALS, is a Department of Homeland Security publication that provides guidance for election officials on how to handle incidents that may occur during an election. The tool includes a description of the types of incidents that may occur, as well as guidelines on how to report and investigate incidents. The tool also includes a list of resources that election officials can use to support their incident handling efforts.
