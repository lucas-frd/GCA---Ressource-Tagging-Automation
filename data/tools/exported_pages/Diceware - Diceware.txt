=Description=
<br>
Diceware is a tool for generating strong, memorable passwords using dice. It works by selecting a random word from a list of 7776 words, then concatenating multiple words together to create a password. The list of words is designed so that each word is equally likely to be selected, and the overall password is thus very difficult to predict.
