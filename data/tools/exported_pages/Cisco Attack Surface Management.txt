Cisco is a leading technology company that provides networking and security solutions to businesses and organizations worldwide. The organization's Attack Surface Management team is dedicated to preventing and mitigating cyber-attacks through a variety of products and services.

The team is an integral part of Cisco's larger Security and Trust organization, which is responsible for protecting customer data, intellectual property, and networks. The Attack Surface Management team works closely with other teams within the Security and Trust organization, such as the Incident Response and Security Operations Center teams
