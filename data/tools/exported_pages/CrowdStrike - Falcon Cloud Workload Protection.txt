Falcon Cloud Workload Protection is an advanced cloud security solution developed by CrowdStrike, a leading cybersecurity company founded in 2011. The organization is headquartered in Sunnyvale, California and has a global presence with offices in the United States, Europe, and Asia.

Falcon Cloud Workload Protection is a comprehensive cloud security platform designed to protect workloads running on AWS, Azure, and GCP (Google Cloud Platform). It offers a wide range of security capabilities, including workload protection, workload visibility
