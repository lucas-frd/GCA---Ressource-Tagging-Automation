Kantega Single Sign-On is a tool that allows you to easily add single sign-on capabilities to your web applications. It integrates with your existing authentication infrastructure and supports a wide range of features. Kantega Single Sign-On is easy to use and provides a high degree of flexibility. It is available as a standalone product or as part of the Kantega Security Suite.
