=Description=
<br>
The Wireshark Plugins site is a SANS Institute tool for capturing and analyzing packets. It can be used to troubleshoot network problems, assess network security, and much more. The site offers a variety of plugins to extend the functionality of Wireshark, as well as a user forum and other resources.
