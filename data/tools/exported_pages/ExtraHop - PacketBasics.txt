=Description=
<br>
ExtraHop PacketBasics is a free, open-source tool that makes it easy to capture and analyze packet data from a network. With PacketBasics, you can see all the traffic on a network, including theFlag=1 packets that are often used to troubleshoot network problems. PacketBasics also provides rich visualization features, so you can see relationships between devices and understand how traffic is flowing through a network.
