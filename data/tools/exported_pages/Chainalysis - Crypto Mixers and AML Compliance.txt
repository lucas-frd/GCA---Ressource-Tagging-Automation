Chainalysis is a company that provides compliance tools for cryptocurrency businesses. One of their tools is called Crypto Mixers, which helps businesses comply with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations. Crypto Mixers allows businesses to track their customers' activity and identify potential money laundering activity. This tool can help businesses avoid fines and penalties associated with AML compliance.
