Smart-1 is an organization based in Check Point, a multinational cyber security and software company founded in Israel in 1993. Smart-1, which is a part of Check Point, is a leading provider of advanced security management solutions for businesses of all sizes. Their primary objective is to simplify and centralize security management, providing businesses with a more efficient and effective way to protect their networks.

The organization's website, https://www.checkpoint.com/products/, showcases their range of products and solutions
