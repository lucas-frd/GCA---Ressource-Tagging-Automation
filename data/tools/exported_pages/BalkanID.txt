=Description=
<br>
BalkanID is a tool that allows users to search for and identify Balkan dialects. Users can search for specific words or phrases, and the results are displayed on a map. The tool also includes a dictionary of Balkan words and phrases, as well as a forum for users to discuss Balkan language and culture.
