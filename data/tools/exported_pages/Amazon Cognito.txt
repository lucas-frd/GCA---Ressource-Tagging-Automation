Amazon Cognito is a user authentication and data synchronization service that helps you securely manage and synchronize user data across devices and applications. With Amazon Cognito, you can easily add user sign-up and sign-in to your mobile and web apps. Amazon Cognito also enables you to save data locally on devices, allowing your applications to work even when offline.
