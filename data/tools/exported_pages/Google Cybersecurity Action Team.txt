The Google Cybersecurity Action Team (G-CAT) is a division of Google that is responsible for finding and responding to security threats. The team uses a variety of tools to find and track security issues, including a database of known security vulnerabilities. The team also develops new tools and technologies to help protect Google users from security threats.
