=Description=
<br>
The SANS Security Policy Templates are a set of customizable security policy documents, designed to help organizations develop and implement comprehensive security programs. The templates address a wide range of security topics, including data security, asset management, access control, incident response, and more.

The templates are designed to be easily adaptable to the specific needs of an organization, and can be used as-is or customized as needed. They can be used to develop security policies for both small and large organizations
