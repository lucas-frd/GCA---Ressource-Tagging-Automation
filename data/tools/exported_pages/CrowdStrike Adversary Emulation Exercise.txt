The CrowdStrike Adversary Emulation Exercise is a service offered by CrowdStrike, a cybersecurity company, designed to help organizations prepare for potential cyber attacks by simulating real-world adversary techniques. This service involves a series of simulated attacks, known as "emulations", that test an organization's defenses and allow them to identify and address any vulnerabilities in their systems.
