Secret Double Octopus is a software company that provides authentication and authorization solutions. Its products enable organizations to secure their data and applications by verifying the identity of users. The company offers a variety of products, including a password manager, a two-factor authentication system, and a fraud detection system. Secret Double Octopus also offers consulting services to help organizations implement its products.
