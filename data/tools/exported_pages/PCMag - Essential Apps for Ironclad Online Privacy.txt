Essential Apps for Ironclad Online Privacy is a curated list of digital tools and applications designed to enhance and safeguard online privacy. This collection was created and is regularly updated by the PCMag editorial team, a reputable technology review website. This organization aims to provide individuals with the necessary resources and information to protect their personal data and maintain a sense of control over their online presence.

The organization's main platform is the 'Essential Apps' article found on the PCMag website.
