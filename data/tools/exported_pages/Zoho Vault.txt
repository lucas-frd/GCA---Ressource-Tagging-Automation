Zoho Vault is a secure, cloud-based password manager that helps businesses keep track of their employee login credentials and other sensitive data. It offers features such as two-factor authentication, password sharing, and activity tracking. Zoho Vault also integrates with other Zoho products, making it a convenient solution for businesses that use multiple Zoho services.
