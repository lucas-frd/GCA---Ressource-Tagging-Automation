=Description=
<br>
The SAP Access Control tool helps organizations manage and monitor access to sensitive data and systems. It provides a centralized view of who has access to what, and when they accessed it. It also provides a way to monitor and control access to specific resources. Organizations can use SAP Access Control to enforce security policies and compliance regulations.
