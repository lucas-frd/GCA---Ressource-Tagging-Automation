The 'Protecting Personal Information: A Guide for Business' is a comprehensive resource provided by the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) to help businesses protect the personal information of their customers. The FTC is a federal agency tasked with protecting consumers from fraudulent and unfair business practices, and promoting competition in the marketplace. The organization's main mission is to prevent business practices that are anti-competitive, deceptive, or fraudulent.
