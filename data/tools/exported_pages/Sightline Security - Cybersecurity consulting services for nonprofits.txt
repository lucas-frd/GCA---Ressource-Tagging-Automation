Sightline Security provides cybersecurity consulting services for nonprofits. Their services include cybersecurity assessments, incident response, and security awareness training. They also offer a free online security assessment tool. This tool helps nonprofits identify and assess their cybersecurity risks. It provides recommendations for improving their security posture.
