=Description=
<br>
NordPass Business is a tool designed to help businesses keep their passwords and other sensitive information safe. It provides a secure vault for storing passwords, as well as a password generator to create strong passwords. NordPass also offers a sharing feature, so businesses can share passwords with team members without having to share the master password. NordPass Business is a subscription-based service, with plans starting at $4 per month per user.
