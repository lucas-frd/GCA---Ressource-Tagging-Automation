Zluri is a cloud-native SaaSOps platform that helps modern enterprises manage their SaaS landscape and identity governance. The platform provides visibility into an organization's SaaS environment, allowing IT and security teams to optimize costs, improve security, and increase productivity. Zluri also helps companies comply with regulations such as GDPR and HIPAA.
