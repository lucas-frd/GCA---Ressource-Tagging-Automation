Elimity is an online tool that helps organizations to improve their discrimination and harassment prevention compliance. The tool consists of a library of customizable policies and procedures, as well as a set of online training modules. Elimity also offers a consulting service to help organizations assess their compliance risks and develop action plans to address those risks.
