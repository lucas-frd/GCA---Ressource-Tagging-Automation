=Description=
<br>
EMDYN BioTrace monitor changes in heart rate, blood pressure, and respiration rate in order to provide feedback for relaxation and stress management. It is a wearable device that can be used for both short-term and long-term monitoring. The device is simple to use and can be worn for extended periods of time. EMDYN BioTrace is an FDA cleared device and has been clinically validated.
