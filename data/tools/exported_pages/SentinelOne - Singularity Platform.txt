SentinelOne is a leading organization that provides cybersecurity solutions through their flagship product, Singularity Platform. This organization is based in Mountain View, California, and was founded in 2013 by Tomer Weingarten and Almog Cohen. With over $1 billion in funding and more than 550 employees globally, SentinelOne has become a trusted name in the cybersecurity industry.

The Singularity Platform is a unified endpoint protection platform that uses artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML)
