The Tor Project is a non-profit organization founded in 2006 that is focused on developing and promoting privacy and anonymity on the internet. The organization is best known for its flagship product, Tor Browser, which is a free and open-source web browser that allows users to browse the internet anonymously and securely.

The Tor Project is an international organization with offices and contributors located around the world. Its headquarters are based in Seattle, Washington, with additional offices in Amsterdam and Berlin.
