=Description=
<br>
Aircrack-ng is a network security tool that can be used to monitor and assess the security of wireless networks. It can be used to crack WEP and WPA-PSK keys, and to sniff traffic. Aircrack-ng can be used to detect wireless intruders, to test the security of wireless networks, and to assess the strength of wireless passwords.
