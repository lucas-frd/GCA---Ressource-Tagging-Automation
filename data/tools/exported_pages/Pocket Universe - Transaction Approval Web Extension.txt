The Transaction Approval Web Extension is a key feature of the Pocket Universe platform, which is a decentralized digital ecosystem built on the Ethereum blockchain. It is developed by the team at Pocket Universe with the goal of simplifying and streamlining the process of approving transactions on the blockchain.

At its core, the Transaction Approval Web Extension is a browser extension that allows users to sign and approve transactions on the Ethereum blockchain directly from their browser. It is compatible with most major browsers, including Chrome, Firefox, and Brave.
