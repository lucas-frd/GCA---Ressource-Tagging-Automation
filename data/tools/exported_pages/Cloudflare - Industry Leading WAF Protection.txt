Industry Leading WAF Protection is a Cloudflare service that provides web application firewall protection. It uses a sophisticated rules engine to protect against a wide range of web application attacks, including SQL injection, cross-site scripting, and session hijacking. It is available as a standalone service or as part of the Cloudflare platform.
