Imprivata OneSign is an authentication and access management tool that enables enterprise single sign-on (SSO) and simplifies user access to applications, devices, and data. The tool can be deployed on-premises or in the cloud, and includes built-in connectors for popular enterprise applications. OneSign provides a high level of security by authenticating users with a variety of methods, including passwords, biometrics, and smart cards. The tool also supports role-based access
