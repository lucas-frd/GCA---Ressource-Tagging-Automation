=Description=
<br>
1Password is a password manager tool that helps users generate and store strong passwords for their online accounts. It can also automatically fill in password fields on websites and sync across devices. 1Password uses AES-256 encryption to keep passwords safe and secure. It also offers a variety of features to help users manage their passwords, such as the ability to create folders, share passwords, and set up security questions. 1Password is available for Mac, Windows, iOS, and Android devices.
