=Description=
<br>
Evidan Identity Governance and Administration is a web-based tool that helps organizations manage and govern their identities. It allows organizations to create and manage identity and access policies, and provides a centralized repository for identity data. It also includes workflow capabilities to help automate and manage access requests and approvals.
