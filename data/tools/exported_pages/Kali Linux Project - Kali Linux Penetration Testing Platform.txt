Kali Linux is a Debian-derived Linux distribution designed for digital forensics and penetration testing. It is free and open source software developed by Offensive Security. The tool contains a large collection of security-related tools divided into categories. The categories include information gathering, vulnerability analysis, exploitation tools, payload tools, and post-exploitation tools.
