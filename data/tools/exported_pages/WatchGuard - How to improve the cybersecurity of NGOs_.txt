WatchGuard is a global cybersecurity company that offers advanced network security solutions for small and midsize enterprises (SMEs) and NGOs. Founded in 1996, the company has over 80,000 customers worldwide and a strong presence in the cybersecurity industry.

The primary goal of WatchGuard is to provide effective and reliable cybersecurity solutions to NGOs, who often have limited resources and face unique security challenges.
