Nozomi Networks is an organization that provides cybersecurity solutions for industrial control systems (ICS) and operational technology (OT) networks. The company was founded in 2013 and is based in October, Switzerland, with additional offices in the United States, United Arab Emirates, Italy, and Australia.

The central focus of Nozomi Networks is to protect critical infrastructure and industrial operations from cyber threats and attacks. Their platform offers advanced threat detection, network visibility, and asset inventory management for industrial control systems
