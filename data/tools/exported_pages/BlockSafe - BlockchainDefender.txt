BlockchainDefender is a BlockSafe Technologies product designed to protect blockchains from attack. It is a Hardware Wallet that stores your private keys offline and signs transactions offline, so that your keys can never be stolen by hackers. It also has a built-in screen so that you can visually confirm each transaction before signing it.
