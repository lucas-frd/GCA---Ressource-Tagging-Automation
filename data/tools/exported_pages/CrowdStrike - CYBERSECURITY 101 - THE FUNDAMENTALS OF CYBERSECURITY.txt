CrowdStrike's Cybersecurity 101: The Fundamentals of Cybersecurity is an online resource that covers the basics of cybersecurity. The site includes articles on topics such as email security, passwords, and two-factor authentication. There is also a glossary of terms, a FAQ section, and a resources section with links to further reading.
