Spiceworks is a comprehensive IT management software that helps businesses in a variety of ways. It gives businesses the ability to inventory and track their hardware and software, monitor their networks, and resolve issues. In addition, Spiceworks provides a marketplace where businesses can find and compare products and services, and connect with IT professionals.
