=Description=
<br>
QlikVerify is a tool that allows users to verify the accuracy of their data within a Qlik application. The tool compares the data in the Qlik application to the data in the source system, and highlights any discrepancies. This allows users to ensure that their data is up-to-date and accurate, and that their Qlik application is functioning correctly.
