=Description=
<br>
RapidIdentity is a software solution that helps organizations manage identities and access to resources. It includes a number of features to streamline identity management, including user provisioning, password management, and Single Sign-On (SSO). RapidIdentity can be deployed on-premises or in the cloud, and it integrates with a variety of third-party applications and systems.
