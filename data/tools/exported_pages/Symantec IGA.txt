Symantec Identity Governance and Administration (IGA) is a comprehensive identity management solution that helps organizations effectively govern the access and use of critical resources across the enterprise. It provides a centralized platform for managing user identities, roles, and entitlements, as well as providing real-time visibility into who has access to what. IGA also helps organizations meet compliance requirements by providing detailed audit logs and reports.
