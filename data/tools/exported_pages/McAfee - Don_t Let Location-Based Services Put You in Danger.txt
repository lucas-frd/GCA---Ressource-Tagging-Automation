Don't Let Location-Based Services Put You in Danger is an informative and educational blog that is part of McAfee's family safety program. It focuses on raising awareness about the potential dangers of using location-based services and providing tips on how to protect oneself while using such services.

McAfee is a global computer security software company headquartered in Santa Clara, California. With over 7,500 employees, it is one of the largest cybersecurity companies in the world, serving both individual consumers and businesses.
