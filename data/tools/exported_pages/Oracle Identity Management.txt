=Description=
<br>
The Oracle Identity Management tool is a centralized, comprehensive solution for managing users' identities, access, and compliance across an organization. It provides a single location to manage all identity and access related activity, and includes a wide range of features such as user provisioning, role-based access control, identity governance, and auditing. The tool is designed to work with a variety of enterprise applications and systems, and can be deployed on-premises or in the cloud.
