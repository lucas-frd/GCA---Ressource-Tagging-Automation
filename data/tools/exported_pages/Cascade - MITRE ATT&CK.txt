=Description=
<br>
Cascade is a tool developed by MITRE that allows users to visualize and analyze data from the MITRE ATT&CK Framework. Cascade allows users to see how their organization stacks up against the ATT&CK Framework and can help identify gaps in their defenses. Cascade is a web-based tool that is available for free.
