Cloudflare is a leading technology company that provides security and performance solutions for websites and applications. As part of their services, Cloudflare offers SSL/TLS encryption to ensure secure communication between websites and their visitors.

SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) and TLS (Transport Layer Security) are cryptographic protocols that establish a secure connection between a web server and a browser. This encryption allows for the transfer of sensitive information, such as personal data and credit card numbers, without the fear of interception by unauthorized parties.
