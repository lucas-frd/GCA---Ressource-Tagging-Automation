=Description=
<br>
Remnux is a SANS Institute tool that is used to analyze and reverse engineer malware. It is a Linux distribution that is specifically designed for this purpose. It includes a number of tools that can be used to dissect malware and understand how it works. This knowledge can then be used to develop countermeasures and defenses against it.
