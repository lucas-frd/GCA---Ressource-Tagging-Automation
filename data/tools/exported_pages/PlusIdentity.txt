PlusIdentity is a tool that allows businesses to manage, track, and secure their online identities. The tool provides a single login for all of your business’s online accounts, making it easy to keep track of your passwords and reduce the risk of identity theft. PlusIdentity also provides two-factor authentication and encryption to help keep your business’s online information safe and secure.
