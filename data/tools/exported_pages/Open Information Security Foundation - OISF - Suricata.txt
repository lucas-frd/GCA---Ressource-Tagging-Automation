Suricata is a open source tool used for Intrusion Detection and Prevention (IDS/IPS). It was created by the Open Information Security Foundation (OISF). It is used to detect and prevent various types of attacks, such as denial of service (DoS), malware, and zero-day attacks. Suricata can be used on various platforms, such as Linux, Windows, andmacOS.
