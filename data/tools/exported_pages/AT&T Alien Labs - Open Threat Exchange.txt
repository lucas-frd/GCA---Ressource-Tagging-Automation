Open Threat Exchange (OTX) is a collaborative security platform founded by AT&T Alien Labs. It is a community-driven, open-source cyber threat intelligence platform aimed at providing real-time threat intelligence feeds and global insights on existing and emerging cyber threats.

OTX is based in AT&T Alien Labs, a research lab focusing on threat intelligence, data science, and security operations. It was founded in 2013 and is headquartered in San Mateo, California.
